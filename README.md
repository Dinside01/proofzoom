# ProofZoom

ProofZoom is a mathematics archive of essays designed to present
mathematical ideas with clarity, structure, and conceptual insight.

Each entry is a carefully written exposition designed to clarify
mathematical arguments whose intermediate reasoning may not always
be explicit in standard presentations. In many mathematical texts,
certain steps in a derivation are left for the reader to reconstruct.
While this practice can strengthen understanding for experienced
readers, it can also present a pedagogical obstacle for many others.
ProofZoom focuses on such arguments—especially those involving
foundational concepts on which many results depend—and presents them
with the intermediate reasoning made fully transparent.
The project emphasizes clarity of reasoning, conceptual organization,
and accessibility for students and mathematicians who do not specialize in the specific area.

## Structure of the Repository

The site is organized around a collection of mathematical entries.
Each entry is an essay devoted to explaining a mathematical idea
with clarity and structural insight.

Key folders include:

- `_entries/` — individual mathematical essays (ProofZoom entries)
- `_layouts/` — page layouts used to render entries and indexes
- `entries/` — the entries index page
- `areas/` — pages for major areas of mathematics
- `assets/` — stylesheets and site resources

## Scholarly Discussion (Planned)

ProofZoom is intended to support scholarly interaction around its
mathematical essays.

Two kinds of discussion spaces are planned.

**Entry discussion.**  
Each essay will eventually include a discussion space where readers
can offer comments, corrections, references, and suggestions for
improving the exposition.

**General discussion.**  
A broader forum will allow mathematicians and students to propose
topics that deserve a ProofZoom-style exposition or to discuss
general questions about the project.

These discussion systems are part of the long-term development of
ProofZoom and are documented in `PROOFZOOM-MANUAL.md`.
